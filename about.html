Who Are We ?

Smartrack Africa is a leading provider of advanced tracking and protection services for vehicles and valuable assets. The company specializes in theft prevention, real-time vehicle location monitoring, and comprehensive asset tracking capabilities. Smartrack's solutions leverage GPS, telematics, and wireless technologies to deliver enhanced security, improve fleet management, and provide detailed insights into the status and movements of vehicles and equipment. By offering a comprehensive platform that combines security systems, real-time tracking, and customizable reporting, Smartrack helps businesses and individuals optimize the management and protection of their mobile resources and physical assets.
